ABOUT

Madi Toktarov is a performer and sound producer. He began his musical career with the creation of instrumentals.

In 2018 he released a solo album “Almaty Jazz Vol. 1 ”, which reveals a retro sound. In 2019, the second part of "Almaty Jazz" comes out, where the artist brings the disco and funk of the eighties to the fore.

In 2019 he will debut on the Musica36 label, with the FEROMON album, which is dominated by the R&B sound of the 2000s.
